Digital Literacy
Overview
Digital literacy is the ability to effectively find, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technologies. It goes beyond just technical skills to include critically thinking about the content you encounter online. Cybersafety, or online safety, is the practice of protecting yourself and your information from digital threats like scams, malware, and cyberbullying. This involves practical steps like using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and managing your privacy settings. In short, digital literacy provides the knowledge to understand online environments, while cybersafety is the action of applying that knowledge to stay secure.
